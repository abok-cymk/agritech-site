The Twiga Process

From soil to sale in
three simple steps

Our technology removes friction at every point in Kenya's food supply chain.

🌱

Farmers List Produce

Smallholder farmers across Kenya list their harvest via SMS or our mobile app — schedules, quantities, pricing — all without leaving their farms.

🚛

Twiga Collects & QA

Our agents collect directly, run quality checks, and route produce through cold-chain logistics to our Nairobi distribution hub.

🏪

Retailers Receive Orders

Urban vendors order via the platform and get doorstep delivery. M-Pesa payment is instant. Both sides win — zero middleman tax.

The Twiga Process

From soil to sale in
three simple steps

Our technology removes friction at every point in Kenya's food supply chain.

🌱

Farmers List Produce

Smallholder farmers across Kenya list their available harvest via SMS or our mobile app. They set schedules, quantities, and pricing — without leaving their farms.

🚛

Twiga Collects & QA

Our agents collect directly from farms, perform quality checks, and route produce through our cold-chain logistics network to our Nairobi distribution hub.

🏪

Retailers Receive Orders

Urban vendors and supermarkets order via our platform and receive doorstep delivery. Payment is seamless via M-Pesa. Both sides win — no middleman tax.

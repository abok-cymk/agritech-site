ℹ️ About JijiMove

JijiMove is Kenya's leading B2B food distribution platform, reimagining how fresh produce moves from farms to urban retailers. We're on a mission to connect smallholder farmers directly to buyers, eliminating intermediaries and building a more equitable food system.

Our Story

Founded in 2026, JijiMove started with a simple observation: Kenya's smallholder farmers were selling at exploitative prices while urban retailers paid premium rates for produce. The middleman was winning, and everyone else was losing.

Today, we've built a platform that directly connects over 5,000 farmers to hundreds of retailers, restaurants, and institutions. We've facilitated over KES 2 billion in direct transactions, ensuring farmers get better prices and retailers get fresher produce.

Our Mission

"Farm to Shelf, Reimagined" — We're building a transparent, efficient, and fair food supply chain where farmers are valued, retailers thrive, and consumers get the freshest produce at competitive prices.

Our Values

  • Transparency: No hidden fees, clear pricing, traceability for every batch
  • Equity: Fair pay for farmers, living wages for all staff
  • Quality: Cold chain excellence and rigorous safety standards
  • Community: Supporting smallholder farmers with training and technology
  • Innovation: Leveraging mobile tech and logistics to solve real problems

Our Impact

  • 5,000+ smallholder farmers empowered with direct market access
  • KES 2 billion+ in direct farmer-to-retailer transactions
  • 40% average price increase for farmers compared to traditional markets
  • 1,200+ retail points served with fresh produce
  • 40 jobs created in logistics and customer support

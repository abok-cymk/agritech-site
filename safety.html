🛡️ Safety Standards

At JijiMove, we're committed to ensuring the highest standards of food safety and security across our entire supply chain. Our comprehensive safety protocols protect farmers, retailers, and consumers.

Cold Chain Management

We maintain strict temperature control throughout the distribution process, ensuring produce stays fresh from farm to retail shelf.

  • Real-time temperature monitoring on all vehicles
  • Insulated packaging with ice packs for optimal freshness
  • Daily quality audits at collection and delivery points

Hygiene & Handling Protocols

Every step of our process adheres to Kenya's food safety regulations and international best practices.

  • Certified food handlers on all teams
  • Regular sanitization of vehicles and equipment
  • Traceability system for every batch
  • Quarterly third-party food safety inspections

Farmer Training & Support

We provide our partner farmers with continuous training on safe farming practices to ensure quality from harvest.

  • Pesticide safety workshops
  • Post-harvest handling certification programs
  • Best practices for organic and conventional farming

Consumer Protection

Your trust is our priority. We offer full transparency and support for any safety concerns.

  • 100% traceability from farm to your shop
  • Money-back guarantee if produce doesn't meet quality standards
  • 24/7 customer support hotline

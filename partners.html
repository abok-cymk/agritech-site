🤝 Our Partners

JijiMove is proud to work with leading organizations across Kenya's agricultural and retail sectors. Our partnerships enable us to deliver fresh produce efficiently while supporting smallholder farmers.

Strategic Partners

Naivas Supermarkets

Kenya's leading supermarket chain, providing fresh JijiMove produce to stores across East Africa.

Farmers Union Cooperative

Supporting over 5,000 smallholder farmers with training, inputs, and direct market access.

Kenya Agricultural Research Institute (KARI)

Collaborating on sustainable farming practices and crop improvement research.

Twiga Foods

Complementary B2B food platform partnership for cross-regional expansion.

Kenya Red Cross

Food security initiatives supporting vulnerable communities with fresh produce programs.

African Development Bank

Funding and technical support for agricultural transformation and supply chain innovation.

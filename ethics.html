🔒 Ethics & Security

JijiMove is built on principles of transparency, fairness, and data security. We believe in ethical business practices that benefit all stakeholders in the agricultural supply chain.

Our Ethical Commitments

Fair Pricing: We guarantee farmers receive at least 85% of the retail price, eliminating exploitative middleman markups.

Living Wages: All JijiMove employees, including logistics partners, earn above Kenya's living wage standard.

Transparency: Every transaction is recorded and visible to both buyer and seller. No hidden fees.

Environmental Stewardship: We incentivize sustainable farming practices and offset our carbon footprint through tree-planting initiatives.

Data Security & Privacy

  • 256-bit encryption for all transactions and personal data
  • Compliance with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019
  • No third-party data sharing without explicit user consent
  • Annual security audits by certified third parties
  • Secure M-Pesa integration with direct bank partnerships

Responsible Supply Chain

  • Zero tolerance for child labor or forced labor
  • Compliance with Kenya's Environmental Management and Coordination Act
  • Support for women farmers with targeted training and market access programs
  • Regular audits of partner farms for labor practices compliance
